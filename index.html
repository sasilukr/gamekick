Ready up for games with friends.
Lobby makes it easy to set up game sessions with friends any day of the week.

Say goodbye to spammy group chats and "maybe" responses. With Lobby, one person creates an event and invites friends. If enough people ready up, everyone gets notified that the event is going down.

Launching soon, sign up for updates.

Convenient
Whether you're at work, at home, or on the go, opting into an event is simple. You'll receive a notification if it's happening and you can forget about it if not. No sweat.
Play more games
With open-invitation events, you're always likely to get enough players to try out that game you just got on sale. Don't waste time trying to get everyone online, just play!
